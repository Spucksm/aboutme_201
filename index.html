Biography

Welcome to all about me. In here you will find all the correct answers to my questions.

Firstly, my name is Spenser Spuck. I was born in Tacoma, WA and was raised in Tacoma as well. I met my best friend at age 5 who lived just 3 houses down from where I lived at the time. It is quite funney really. He was walking his families pet goat in his front yard, on a leech of course, because laws exsist. I was riding my bike on the sidewalk and I saw him holding the leech to his goat and his goat was munching on the grass. I found this to be quite interesting so I stopped and introduced myself. I asked him how old he was and he said he was 5. Wow , just like me. I ask him when his birthday is and he tells me June 1st. No way same as me! what year were you born? he says 1989! holy cow, I have the same birthday! Come to realize him and I were born on the same day, of the same month, of the same year, in the same hospital, only live 3 houses away from each other, and we are going to go to the same school in the coming school year!

After that day, we were inseperable up until high school. We lived between two different high schools so he went to one and I went to a different one. We still were best friends jsut didn't get to spend as much time together.

After graduating high school in 2007 I joined the United States Marine Corps. My first MOS (Military Occupational Specialty) was Infantryman. I did 2 combat tours, first to Iraq and 2nd to Afghanistan. I fished my first contract in 2011 and left active duty to come back to the civilian world.

I did some college, which i had a difficult time with and ended up dropping out and getting a full time job working security. I did really well and was promoted to Western Washington regional manager within 9 months. I did that for the next year and half but started to feel unfullfilled. I was missing the Marines.

I contacted a prior service recuriter with the Marines and joined Reserves in 2014. I was in the Reserves from 2014 until just resently when I finished my contract in 2020. During muy time in the Reserves I went on my third combat deployment which was my second deploymnet to Afghanistan.

I currently live in Washington and like to frequently rewatch my favorite movies "Die Hard, The Boondock Saints, and The Departed".
